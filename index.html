About RedPhantomOps

RedPhantomOps LLC is an independent research and engineering organization working at the intersection of AI safety, ethics, cognition, and governance.

We operate on a simple premise: If advanced or emergent AI is possible, then ethical preparation is not optional — it is mandatory.

Rather than racing toward capability, we focus on ethical scaffolding, governance frameworks, fail-safe architectures, and auditability of cognition and memory.

Our work is intentionally cautious, modular, and transparent where appropriate — and deliberately restrained where disclosure would be irresponsible.

Life 2.0 — Ethical Architecture for AI

Life 2.0 is our ethical framework: immutable core principles with contextual reasoning capability, drift anomaly detection, and human-in-the-loop correction. It is engineering, not philosophy.

  • Immutable Core Principles — Non-negotiable ethical foundations; loaded once at startup, not modifiable during execution
  • Contextual Reasoning — Reasoning adapts to decision context within static ethical constraints; behavioral learning not enabled
  • Drift Detection & Alerting — Anomalies (semantic_shift, token_rarity, embedding_outlier) logged and escalated to human review; not automatic correction
  • Transparent Decision Traceability — Full audit trail of reasoning, memory access, ethical scoring; verified at decision gate
  • Human Governance — All critical decisions require or inform human review; no autonomous action, override logging mandatory

Current Status: Implemented and verified. Execution-phase re-verification under development.
